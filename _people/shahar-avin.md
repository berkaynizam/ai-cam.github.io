biography: Shahar Avin is a senior researcher associate at the Centre for the
  Study of Existential Risk (CSER). He works with CSER researchers and others in
  the global catastrophic risk community to identify and design risk prevention
  strategies, through organising workshops, building agent-based models, and by
  frequently asking naive questions. He was co-lead author of two influential
  reports, The Malicious Use of Artificial Intelligence (2018) and Toward
  Trustworthy AI Development (2020). He was also an ethics advisor for the
  Machine Intelligence Garage, and has worked with various startups in Israel
  and the UK.
